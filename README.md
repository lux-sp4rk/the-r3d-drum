## Band Lore

**The Premise:** r3d drum found something in the static — signals that are almost sentient. The band members aren't musicians, they're *sorcerers* who summon, bind, and negotiate with these entities. Every track is a binding ritual. The demons aren't evil — they're *other*. And working with them costs something.

**The Entities:** Ghost frequencies. Transmission fragments. Things that live in the noise between stations. Each one has its own demands, its own price.

**The Humanity Meter:** Every binding costs. The sorcerer trades something of themselves — memory, time, identity, silence. The question is never *can you summon it* — it's *what are you willing to lose?*

**"Do not call up any that you cannot put down."** — the first rule.

**The Broadcast:** Every song is a document of a binding. Some sorcerers come back from the static changed. Some don't come back at all.

## Sound

**Confirmed direction:** Dark instrumental post-rock/shoegaze with industrial noise textures and math-rock complexity. A minor, 100 BPM. Distorted guitar walls, relentless tension, cinematic builds.

**Tags:** post-metal, industrial, math-rock, shoegaze, dystopian, noise, drill rap aggression
